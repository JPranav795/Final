What’s in Dr. NUT?

Great question! Dr. NUT is a blend of carbonated water, natural nut extracts, a touch of sweetness, and a whole lot of love. We use real almonds, hazelnuts, peanuts, and more, sourced from the best farms. No artificial junk here—just pure, nutty goodness. Want the full ingredient list? It’s on every can, but we’ll break it down for you over the next 10 paragraphs if you keep reading!

Is Dr. NUT healthy?

Absolutely! We’ve designed it to be low in sugar, high in flavor, and packed with nutty nutrients. It’s not a meal replacement, but it’s a darn good snack alternative. Our nutritionists have spent years ensuring it’s a guilt-free treat. Want more details? We’ve got a 20-page report we could share—stay tuned!

Are Dr. Nut products safe for people with nut allergies?

Dr. Nut products are made with the finest nuts, so they’re not suitable for those with nut allergies. We take allergens seriously and clearly label all our packaging to keep you informed. If you’re looking for nut-free options, we recommend checking out our friends in the seed or fruit snack world—but we’ll stick to what we do best: celebrating nuts!
